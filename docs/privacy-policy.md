# Privacy Policy — Didi

**Last updated: 16 June 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Didi ("Didi", "we", "us") collects, uses, and
shares information when you use the Didi mobile app and website at
https://ghdidi.com (the "Service"). Didi is a food‑ordering platform connecting
customers with local kitchens and restaurants in Ghana.

By using the Service you agree to this policy.

---

## Information we collect

**Information you provide**
- **Account details (restaurant owners):** name, email address, phone number,
  restaurant name, and password.
- **Order details (customers):** name, phone number, optional email, delivery
  address and notes. Customers can order without creating an account.
- **Messages** you send to a restaurant through the app.

**Information collected automatically**
- **Location:** with your permission, your device location is used to find
  kitchens near you and to calculate delivery fees. You can decline; some
  features will be limited.
- **Device & push tokens:** a push notification token and basic device/platform
  information, used to deliver order updates.
- **Usage data:** basic, non‑identifying information about how the app is used,
  to keep it reliable.

**Payment information**
- Payments are processed by **ExpressPay**, a licensed Ghanaian payment
  provider. Card and Mobile Money details are entered with ExpressPay and are
  **not stored by Didi**. We receive only a payment confirmation and reference.

We do **not** use third‑party advertising networks and do **not** track you
across other companies' apps or websites.

---

## How we use your information

- To take, process, and deliver your orders.
- To calculate delivery fees and estimated times based on location.
- To send order updates and important notices by push notification, SMS, or
  email (for example: order confirmed, prepared, on the way, or cancelled).
- To provide restaurant owners with their dashboard, orders, and analytics.
- To keep the Service secure, prevent fraud, and meet legal obligations.

---

## How information is shared

We share information only as needed to run the Service:

- **With the restaurant** you order from (your name, phone, delivery address, and
  order details) so they can prepare and deliver your order.
- **Service providers** that operate the platform on our behalf:
  - **Supabase** — secure database and authentication (hosting your data).
  - **ExpressPay** — payment processing.
  - **Firebase Cloud Messaging / Apple Push Notification service** — push
    notifications.
  - **SMS and email providers** — order and account notifications.
- **Legal reasons** — if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and
  security.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

---

## Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to
provide the Service, resolve disputes, and comply with legal obligations. You can
request deletion of your account and associated personal data (see "Your rights").

---

## Security

We use industry‑standard measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and
access controls, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage
is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and respond to incidents.

---

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to
withdraw permissions such as location or notifications (you can also change these
in your device settings). To make a request, contact us at the email below and we
